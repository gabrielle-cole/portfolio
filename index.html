gabrielle cole is a jamaican-canadian writer residing in montreal. she holds a BA in english literature from mcgill university, and is currently pursuing her MA in creative writing at concordia university.

In her spare time, she is the comics editor at carte blanche magazine, co-editor of chouette magazine, and a juror and assistant coordinator at twelve vacancies film festival.

her two great loves are j.d. salinger and cosmopolitans.
